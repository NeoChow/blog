Privacy Policy

What information do I collect?

The following is a complete list of all the information I may collect.

  • IP address

    Why: To get a very rough sense of where people are reading my blog from.

    When: Whenever you load a page on this site.

    How long: Indefinitely

  • Refering Site

    Why: To discover who is linking to my blog and how much traffic it is driving.

    When: Whenever you click a link that directs you here.

    How long: Indefinitely

  • Subscriber's Email Address

    Why: To allow sending notifications about new blog posts to those who want to receive them.

    When: When you submit your email in the subscription form.

    How long: Until you choose to unsubscribe at which point your email will be completely erased from our database.
